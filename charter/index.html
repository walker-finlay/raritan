Liberty Landing Marina

https://libertylandingmarina.com/

Jersey City's Liberty Landing Marina is located in beautiful Liberty State Park. The Marine Center provides boat services and is home to a marine store and the Liberty House Restaurant, an elegant dining experience.

The Liberty Landing Ferry is minutes away and provides fast & affordable access to New York City if you don't feel like sailing.

One day NY Harbor Cruise:

Leave Liberty Landing mid-morning for leisurely sail south toward Varrazano Narrows Bridge. Sail back into harbor, approaching close to Statue of Liberty. Anchor near statue for lunch. Set sail north after lunch toward George Washington Bridge. Turn south again late in the afternoon, sailing slowly past West side of Manhattan as sun sets. Have dinner underway as the boat swings around the southern tip of Manhattan. Sail past South Street Seaport and motor under the Brooklyn Bridge and Manhattan Bridge. After viewing United Nations and East Side, head back to Liberty Landing, taking in the spectacular sights of Downtown at night.
